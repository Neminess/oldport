About me

My name is Noora Hiltunen and I am a student at Taitotalo.

At Kuntoutussäätiö

During my time at Kuntoutussäätiö(which can be best described as a "rehabilitation/training centre for unemployed people", I got introduced to HTML/CSS, video-editing and photography.
I learned how a basic HTML-page works and what CSS does. I created my own little projects, with the help of internet. My time at Kuntoutussäätiö helped me get the basics of web-design, mostly static pages.
One thing that helped me learn there, was the staff. They were extremely encouraging, friendly and all-around great people who helped me start the journey to the world of coding. Eventually I moved on from Kuntoutussäätiö and applied to Tietix.

Tietix

Tietix is a workshop focused on computers, although they do have a packing-faction as well. There the customers package incoming merch that is then sent to Kierrätyskeskus and similar places for sale.
I went to the coding-/computer-side of Tietix. There, I was taught the basics of Python, got a small list of resources to use for studying and practice-projects. Tietix is the main reason for my current situation. I never thought I could be a coder, but the encouragement given there, and at Kuntoutussäätiö, gave me the kick to start studying more.
When I had to leave Tietix, I applied to two different schools: Omnia and Taitotalo. I wasn't accepted to Omnia, but got a place at Taitotalo (which, funny enough, is an organisation that came to be from the unification of AmiEdu and AEL. Both are my former schools).
All in all, it's been a somewhat long road to get here and I can wholeheartedly say that, without those two places that helped me find my own way, I wouldn't be where I am now.
